The Human Behind the Designs

That's me, thinking about landscape architecture

Architect. Dreamer. Crocheter.

Hi there! I'm Chandini, a 25-year-old architect with a passion for landscape architecture and spaces that tell stories. I believe architecture should be both functional and poetic — creating experiences, not just structures.

My approach combines technical precision with creative exploration, drawing inspiration from literature, cinema, and my love for crocheting (yes, really!).

✨ When I'm not drafting designs, you'll find me with a crochet hook, lost in a Murakami novel, or sketching scenes from my favorite K-drama.

What Inspires Me

Literature

Murakami's surrealism and Calvino's imaginary cities fuel my design process.

Cinema

Wong Kar-wai's visual poetry and Wes Anderson's symmetry influence my spatial thinking.

Landscape

Creating spaces that harmonize with and enhance the natural environment.

Crafting

Crochet patterns and textile arts inform my understanding of structure and texture.
