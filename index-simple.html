The Creative Minimalist

Hey, I'm Chandini. I see architecture as a canvas for stories, not just structures. At 25, I'm finding my own rhythm balancing big dreams with my love for the simple things. When I'm not sketching buildings, you might find me curled up with a novel, paintbrush in hand, quietly crocheting, or lost in the latest K-drama.

My design philosophy? Less is more. Clean lines, thoughtful spaces, a touch of playfulness—and just enough creative chaos to keep things interesting (blame that on my occasionally lazy Sunday afternoons).

Philosophy

"In my architecture and art, I embrace both structure and imperfection. Like the buildings I design, I'm a work in progress—ambitious yet appreciating slow mornings, determined yet occasionally distracted by a good K-drama. This balance of discipline and playfulness shapes my creativity and everything I create."
